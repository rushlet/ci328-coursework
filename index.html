Don't Panic

It is your mission to navigate through outer space, collecting coins and avoiding alien abductions - they could be Vogons trying to find an unsuspecting audience for their abominable poetry.
Like the Heart of Gold, the rocket has an Infinite Improbability Drive - when it's ready to be activated, it will appear in the bottom right hand corner. Clicking on it might cause anything from regeneration into a different galaxy to a whale being spontaneously called into existence.

How To Play

Tap on the left hand side of your screen to move left and the right to move right.

Use your left and right directional buttons to move.
