Don't Panic

It is your mission to navigate through outer space, collecting coins and avoiding alien abductions.
You are armed with a Random Reassignment Button - when it's ready to be activated, it will appear in the bottom right hand corner. Clicking on it could trigger regeneration into a different galaxy or maybe even the spontaneous apparition of a bowl of flowers...

How To Play

Tap on the left hand side of your screen to move left and the right to move right.

Use your left and right directional buttons to move.
